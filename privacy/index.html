Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 28, 2026 | Developer: Blackwall Studio

What data does the app collect?

Clean Notepad (Copper, Silver, and Gold editions) stores your notes locally on your device only. The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data storage

All notes are stored in a local SQLite database on your device. Settings (such as theme preference) are stored in local DataStore preferences. No data leaves your device.

Network access

Clean Notepad does not require internet access. The app does not communicate with any servers, analytics services, or third-party services.

Third-party services

The app contains a voluntary "Buy Me a Coffee" link that opens your web browser. This is entirely optional and takes you to an external website governed by its own privacy policy.

Permissions

Clean Notepad requests no runtime permissions. It operates entirely within its own app sandbox.

Data deletion

Uninstalling the app removes all stored data. You can also delete individual notes within the app, which are moved to trash and permanently purged after 30 days.

Children's privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any data from children. Since no data is collected from any user, there is no special handling required.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.
